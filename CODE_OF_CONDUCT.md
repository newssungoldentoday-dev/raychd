## 🌐 ENGLISH VERSION

### Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers of **Ray Chad** pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

### Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping newcomers and beginners
- Giving credit where credit is due

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Spam or flooding IRC channels
- Cyberbullying or online harassment (violates RA 10175)
- Sharing illegal content under Philippine law
- Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

---

### Enforcement Responsibilities

BlueBixt Ltd maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

---

### Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces including:

- GitHub repository (Issues, Pull Requests, Discussions)
- Ray Chad IRC channels
- Ray Chad Forum
- Any official Ray Chad social media or communication channels

---

### Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to BlueBixt Ltd via:

📧 **GitHub Issues:** https://github.com/newssungoldentoday-dev/raychad/issues

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

---

### Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers will follow these guidelines in determining consequences:

**1. Correction**
- **Offense:** Minor inappropriate language or behavior
- **Consequence:** Private warning with explanation

**2. Warning**
- **Offense:** A violation through a single incident or series of actions
- **Consequence:** Public warning and temporary restriction from interaction

**3. Temporary Ban**
- **Offense:** Serious violation of community standards
- **Consequence:** Temporary ban from IRC channels and GitHub interactions

**4. Permanent Ban**
- **Offense:** Repeated violations, harassment, or illegal activity under Philippine law
- **Consequence:** Permanent ban from all Ray Chad community spaces

---

### Philippine Law Compliance

As BlueBixt Ltd is based in **Baguio City, Philippines**, this Code of Conduct is also governed by:

- **RA 10175** — Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012
- **RA 10173** — Data Privacy Act of 2012
- **RA 11313** — Safe Spaces Act (Bawal Bastos Law)

Violations of these laws will be reported to the appropriate Philippine authorities.

## 🇵🇭 BERSYON SA FILIPINO

### Aming Pangako

Kami bilang mga miyembro, kontribyutor, at tagapanatili ng **Ray Chad** ay nangangako na gawing libre sa harassment ang pakikilahok sa aming komunidad para sa lahat, anuman ang edad, sukat ng katawan, kapansanan, etnikidad, katangiang pansexo, pagkakakilanlan at pagpapahayag ng kasarian, antas ng karanasan, edukasyon, katayuang sosyo-ekonomiko, nasyonalidad, personal na hitsura, lahi, kasta, kulay, relihiyon, o sekswal na pagkakakilanlan at oryentasyon.

Nangangako kaming kumilos at makipag-ugnayan sa mga paraang nag-aambag sa isang bukas, maligayang, magkakaibang, inklusibo, at malusog na komunidad.

---

### Aming mga Pamantayan

Mga halimbawa ng pag-uugaling nag-aambag sa positibong kapaligiran:

- Paggamit ng mapagkomang at inklusibong wika
- Paggalang sa iba't ibang pananaw at karanasan
- Maayos na pagtanggap ng mapag-aaral na kritisismo
- Pagtuon sa kung ano ang pinakamabuti para sa komunidad
- Pagpapakita ng empatiya sa ibang miyembro ng komunidad
- Pagtulong sa mga baguhan at nagsisimula
- Pagbibigay ng kredito kung saan ito nararapat

Mga halimbawa ng hindi katanggap-tanggap na pag-uugali:

- Paggamit ng seksuwalisadong wika o imahen
- Panggugulo, pang-iinsulto, o mapang-aping mga komento
- Personal o pulitikal na pag-atake
- Pampubliko o pribadong harassment
- Pag-publish ng pribadong impormasyon ng iba nang walang pahintulot
- Spam o pagbaha ng mga IRC channel
- Cyberbullying o online harassment (lumalabag sa RA 10175)
- Pagbabahagi ng ilegal na nilalaman sa ilalim ng batas ng Pilipinas
- Anumang iba pang pag-uugali na makatuwirang itinuturing na hindi angkop

---

### Mga Responsibilidad sa Pagpapatupad

Ang mga tagapanatili ng BlueBixt Ltd ay responsable sa paglilinaw at pagpapatupad ng aming mga pamantayan ng katanggap-tanggap na pag-uugali at gagawa ng angkop at makatarungang pagwawasto bilang tugon sa anumang pag-uugaling itinuturing nilang hindi angkop, nagbabanta, nakakasakit, o nakakasama.

---

### Saklaw

Lahat ng reklamo ay susuriin at iimbestigahan nang mabilis at patas.

---

- **RA 10175** — Cybercrime Prevention Act of 2012
- **RA 10173** — Data Privacy Act of 2012
- **RA 11313** — Safe Spaces Act (Bawal Bastos Law)

Ang mga paglabag sa mga batas na ito ay iuulat sa mga naaangkop na awtoridad ng Pilipinas.
