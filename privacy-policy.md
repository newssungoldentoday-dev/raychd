## 🌐 ENGLISH VERSION

### 1. Introduction
Ray Chad is an open-source, self-hosted IRC chat application developed and maintained by **BlueBixt Ltd**, located at **Teachers Camp, Baguio City, Philippines**. This Privacy Policy is crafted in compliance with the **Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173)** and its Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR), enforced by the **National Privacy Commission (NPC)** of the Philippines.

---

### 2. Our Commitment
BlueBixt Ltd is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with RA 10173. We uphold the following principles:
- **Transparency** — We inform you of how your data is used.
- **Legitimate Purpose** — Data is collected only for lawful purposes.
- **Proportionality** — We only collect data necessary for the service.

---

### 3. Information We Collect
Ray Chad may collect the following personal data:

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Nickname / Username | To identify you in IRC channels |
| IP Address | For server connection and security |
| Chat Messages | To operate the IRC chat service |
| Connection Logs | For troubleshooting and server maintenance |

We do **not** collect:
- Government-issued ID numbers
- Financial or payment information
- Precise location data
- Sensitive personal information as defined under RA 10173

---

### 4. Legal Basis for Processing
Under RA 10173, we process your data based on:
- **Consent** — by using Ray Chad, you consent to this policy
- **Legitimate Interest** — to operate and secure the IRC server
- **Compliance with Legal Obligations** — as required by Philippine law

---

### 5. How We Use Your Information
Your data is used solely to:
- Operate and maintain the Ray Chad IRC server
- Ensure the security of the server and its users
- Comply with lawful orders from Philippine authorities

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties without your consent, except when required by Philippine law.

---

### 6. Data Retention
Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for its purpose or as required by Philippine law. Server logs are deleted regularly by the server administrator.

---

### 7. Your Rights Under RA 10173
As a data subject, you have the following rights:

- **Right to be Informed** — Know how your data is being processed
- **Right to Access** — Request a copy of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification** — Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure** — Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Object** — Object to processing of your data
- **Right to Data Portability** — Receive your data in a usable format
- **Right to Lodge a Complaint** — File a complaint with the NPC

To exercise your rights, contact us at the details in Section 11.

---

### 8. Data Security
BlueBixt Ltd implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse, in accordance with RA 10173.

### 10. Children's Privacy
Ray Chad is not intended for users under **13 years of age**. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Parents or guardians may contact us to request deletion of a child's data.

## 🇵🇭 BERSYON SA FILIPINO

### 1. Panimula
Ang Ray Chad ay isang open-source, self-hosted na IRC chat application na binuo at pinapanatili ng **BlueBixt Ltd**, na matatagpuan sa **Teachers Camp, Baguio City, Pilipinas**. Ang Patakaran sa Privacy na ito ay ginawa alinsunod sa **Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173)** at ang mga Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR) nito, na ipinapatupad ng **National Privacy Commission (NPC)** ng Pilipinas.

---

### 2. Aming Pangako
Ang BlueBixt Ltd ay nakatuon sa pagprotekta ng iyong personal na data alinsunod sa RA 10173. Sinusunod namin ang mga sumusunod na prinsipyo:
- **Transparency** — Ipinapaalam namin sa iyo kung paano ginagamit ang iyong data.
- **Lehitimong Layunin** — Kinokolekta ang data para lamang sa mga lehitimong layunin.
- **Proporsyonalidad** — Kinokolekta lamang namin ang data na kailangan para sa serbisyo.

---

### 3. Impormasyong Kinokolekta Namin
Maaaring kolektahin ng Ray Chad ang sumusunod na personal na data:

| Data | Layunin |
|---|---|
| Palayaw / Username | Para makilala ka sa mga IRC channel |
| IP Address | Para sa koneksyon ng server at seguridad |
| Mga Chat Message | Para patakbuhin ang serbisyo ng IRC chat |
| Mga Log ng Koneksyon | Para sa troubleshooting at pagpapanatili ng server |

Hindi namin kinokolekta ang:
- Mga numero ng ID na inilabas ng gobyerno
- Impormasyon sa pananalapi o pagbabayad
- Tumpak na data ng lokasyon
- Sensitibong personal na impormasyon ayon sa kahulugan ng RA 10173

---

### 4. Legal na Batayan ng Pagpoproseso
Sa ilalim ng RA 10173, pinoproseso namin ang iyong data batay sa:
- **Pahintulot** — sa paggamit ng Ray Chad, pumapayag ka sa patakarang ito
- **Lehitimong Interes** — upang patakbuhin at i-secure ang IRC server
- **Pagsunod sa Mga Legal na Obligasyon** — ayon sa hinihingi ng batas ng Pilipinas

---

### 5. Paano Namin Ginagamit ang Iyong Impormasyon
Ang iyong data ay ginagamit lamang upang:
- Patakbuhin at panatilihin ang Ray Chad IRC server
- Tiyakin ang seguridad ng server at ng mga gumagamit nito
- Sumunod sa mga legal na utos mula sa mga awtoridad ng Pilipinas

Hindi namin **ibinebenta, inuupahan, o ibinabahagi** ang iyong personal na data sa mga third party nang walang iyong pahintulot, maliban kung kinakailangan ng batas ng Pilipinas.

---

### 6. Pagpapanatili ng Data
Ang personal na data ay pinapanatili lamang hanggang sa kinakailangan para sa layunin nito o ayon sa hinihingi ng batas ng Pilipinas. Ang mga log ng server ay regular na tinatanggal ng server administrator.

---

### 7. Iyong mga Karapatan sa ilalim ng RA 10173
Bilang isang data subject, mayroon kang mga sumusunod na karapatan:

- **Karapatang Maabisuhan** — Malaman kung paano pinoproseso ang iyong data

- **Karapatang Tumutol** — Tumutol sa pagpoproseso ng iyong data
- **Karapatang Data Portability** — Matanggap ang iyong data sa isang magagamit na format
- **Karapatang Magreklamo** — Maghain ng reklamo sa NPC
