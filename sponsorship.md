Ray Chad is a free, open-source, self-hosted IRC chat application built and maintained by **BlueBixt Ltd** located at **Teachers Camp, Baguio City, Philippines**. Ray Chad will always be free — but your sponsorship helps us keep it alive, maintained, and growing!
